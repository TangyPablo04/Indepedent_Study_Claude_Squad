Independent Study · Spring 2026

Measuring AI Aptitude
in Business Education

A research project examining the gap between self-perceived and demonstrated AI readiness among Fowler College of Business students at San Diego State University.

See our work →
01

The Question

Are FCB students actually prepared for an AI-driven workforce — or do they just think they are?

02

The Method

A custom-built survey tool combining Likert self-perception scales, a knowledge check, and AI-scored open-ended business scenarios.

03

The Outcome

Low response rates prevented statistical conclusions — but the tool itself and the framework for future research are the lasting contribution.
